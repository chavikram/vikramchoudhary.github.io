About Me

I am a Scientist working with the Government of India in the field of e-Governance and digital public infrastructure. My work focuses on building large scale ICT platforms that enable citizens to access public services efficiently.

I have contributed to multiple national level projects in healthcare, civil aviation and governance systems.

Present Work

NextGen eHospital Project – National Informatics Centre

I am presently working on the NextGen eHospital Project developed by National Informatics Centre (NIC). The system is a cloud-based Hospital Management Information System designed to digitize hospital workflows including OPD registration, admission, billing, laboratory, radiology and electronic health records.

The platform is built on modern microservice architecture and is integrated with the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission ecosystem to enable nationwide digital healthcare services.

Past Work

CGHS Project

Contributed to digital initiatives for the Central Government Health Scheme (CGHs) which provides healthcare services to central government employees and pensioners across India.

Lifeline UDAN Portal

The Lifeline UDAN portal was developed during the COVID-19 pandemic to coordinate transportation of essential medical supplies through India’s UDAN regional aviation network.

eSahaj Project – Ministry of Civil Aviation

The eSahaj portal enables online submission and processing of security clearance applications required for aviation related services. The system streamlines approvals among multiple government agencies and ministries.

PQSoft – Parliamentary Questions System

PQSoft is an internal government software used for managing parliamentary questions raised in Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha. It helps departments track, prepare and respond to parliamentary queries efficiently.
